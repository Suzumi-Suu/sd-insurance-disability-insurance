Types of Disability Insurance Coverage

At SD Insurance, we offer a range of disability insurance coverage options to ensure comprehensive protection:

  • Short-Term Disability Insurance: Provides coverage for a limited period, typically up to six months.
  • Long-Term Disability Insurance: Offers coverage for extended periods, often until retirement age.
  • Individual Disability Insurance: Tailored to meet the needs of individuals and self-employed professionals.
  • Group Disability Insurance: Provides coverage for employees through employer-sponsored plans.

Benefits of Disability Insurance

Disability insurance provides extensive coverage, protecting you against a variety of risks, including:

  • Loss of income due to disability
  • Medical expenses related to the disability
  • Rehabilitation and therapy costs
  • Support for daily living expenses
